Official Rulebook

EX-CHANGE!

Outsmart the Market. Master the Trade.

Objective

Outsmart the market and maximize your profits. The player with the highest total Victory Points (VP) at the end of the game wins.

Components

30 Stock Cards — Numbered 1 to 30.

Setup

  • 1. The Opening Index: Find the card numbered 15 and place it face-up in the center of the table. This is the Active Pile.
  • 2. Deal: Shuffle the remaining 29 cards and deal Portfolio Cards face-down to each player:
    • — 2 Players: 6 cards per player.
    • — 3 Players: 5 cards per player.
    • — 4 Players: 4 cards per player.
  • 3. The Market (Draw Pile): Place the remaining cards face-down next to the Active Pile.

Gameplay

The game is played in turns, moving clockwise. On your turn, perform the following two steps:

Step 1 — Influence the Market

Play one card from your hand face-up onto the Active Pile. This card becomes the new "Market Price."

Step 2 — Choose Your Strategy

After playing a card, choose ONE of the following two actions:

Option A

Reinvest

Draw the top card from the Draw Pile and add it to your hand. Your turn ends. No points this turn — but your portfolio stays full for future opportunities.

Option B

Cash Out

Score Victory Points equal to the absolute difference between the previous Market Price and the card you just played. Your hand shrinks by one.

The Scoring Formula

| Old Price − New Price | = Victory Points

Game End & Winning

The game continues until one of the following three conditions is met:

  • 1.

    Market Saturation

    Every player Cashes Out all cards from their portfolio.
  • 2.

    Market Liquidation

    The Draw Pile is empty. Remaining players with cards get one final turn to Cash Out one card.
  • 3.

    Last Trader Standing

    If only one player remains with cards, they get one final turn to Cash Out one card.

Final Score

Add all VP from your Cash Out actions — highest total wins the title of Market Master.
