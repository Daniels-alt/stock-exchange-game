How to Play

Theme

You are stock traders on Wall Street. Three stocks are listed on the exchange: Tech, Real Estate, and Energy. Buy low, sell high!

Setup

  • 45 cards total: 3 suits, each numbered 1-15
  • Card 8 of each suit starts face-up as the market price
  • Remaining cards are shuffled into a draw pile
  • Each player receives: 6 cards (2 players), 5 cards (3 players), or 4 cards (4 players)

On Your Turn

  • Play a card: Place any card from your hand on its matching suit pile
  • Then choose:
    • Draw a new card from the draw pile, OR
    • Sell to score victory points equal to the absolute difference between the old pile value and your played card

Game End

  • The game ends when all players have emptied their hands, OR when the draw pile runs out
  • If the draw pile empties, each player with cards gets one final turn to sell
  • The player with the most victory points wins!

Strategy Tip

Playing a card far from the pile's current value earns more points. But it also sets up the pile for the next player...
